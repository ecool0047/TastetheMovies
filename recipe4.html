Boiled Dumplings

Description:

Inspired by Mei's mom's cooking and Pixar's Turning Red. Soft, boiled dumplings filled with pork for a savory treat.

Ingredients

  • For dough: 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • Â¾ cup warm water
  • For filling: ground pork
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Mix dough ingredients and let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  2. Prepare filling by cooking pork or mushrooms with garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  3. Roll dough into small balls, flatten each ball, add filling, and pinch to close.
  4. Steam the buns in a bamboo steamer for 15-20 minutes until fluffy.
