Italian Potato Gnocchi with Butter & Sage

Description:

This classic Italian dish, made with tender gnocchi and a rich butter and sage sauce, is the perfect comfort food, inspired by family dinners in Portorosso.

Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • Butter
  • Fresh sage leaves

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes until tender, then peel and mash.
  2. Mix the mashed potatoes with egg and flour to form a dough.
  3. Roll the dough into ropes, then cut into small gnocchi pieces.
  4. Boil the gnocchi in salted water until they float to the top.
  5. In a pan, brown butter and crisp fresh sage leaves. Toss the gnocchi in the butter and sage.
