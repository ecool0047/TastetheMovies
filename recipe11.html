Hazelnut Soup

Description:

A creamy and hearty vegetable soup with ground hazelnuts, based on medieval European peasant food, as seen in the movie Tangled.

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 potato, peeled and cubed
  • ½ cup ground hazelnuts
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • Cream or milk (to taste)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Thyme

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions and carrots in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add potato and vegetable broth. Simmer until potatoes are soft.
  3. Stir in ground hazelnuts and simmer for 10 more minutes.
  4. Blend the soup until smooth. Add cream or milk to taste.
