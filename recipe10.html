Cuban Sandwich

Description:

A delicious and iconic Miami street food made famous in the movie Chef, with layers of savory roasted pork, ham, Swiss cheese, and pickles, all grilled to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • Cuban bread or French bread
  • Yellow mustard
  • Sliced pickles
  • Swiss cheese
  • Roasted pork
  • Sliced ham
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Slice the bread into sandwich-sized pieces.
  2. Layer mustard, pickles, Swiss cheese, roasted pork, and ham onto one side of the bread.
  3. Top with the other slice of bread.
  4. Butter the outsides of the sandwich.
  5. Press the sandwich in a panini press or skillet until golden and melty.
