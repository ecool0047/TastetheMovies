Traditional Chinese Rice Porridge

Description:

A real, savory breakfast in Asia, featured in the movie Mulan. Simple, hearty, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 7 cups water or broth
  • Salt
  • Toppings: century egg, green onions, shredded chicken, soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly.
  2. In a large pot, bring water or broth to a boil, then reduce the heat to simmer.
  3. Stir occasionally and let it cook for 1 to 1Â½ hours, until the mixture thickens to a porridge-like consistency.
  4. Season with salt to taste.
  5. Add your preferred toppings like century egg, green onions, shredded chicken, and a drizzle of soy sauce.
