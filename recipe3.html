Colombian Cheese-Stuffed Arepas

Description:

Mirabel's mom heals with these—emotionally and literally! A comforting and delicious dish from Colombia in the movie Encanto.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups masarepa (precooked cornmeal, like PAN)
  • 1½ cups warm water
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella or queso blanco
  • Salt
  • Butter or oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix the masarepa, warm water, and salt to form dough. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Shape the dough into patties and stuff each with cheese inside.
  3. Cook the patties on a greased skillet for 5-7 minutes per side until golden and the cheese is melty inside.
