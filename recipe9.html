Creamy Butterscotch Soda Drink (Non-Alcoholic)

Description:

This deliciously sweet drink is inspired by the magical *Butterbeer* from the *Harry Potter* series. Itâ€™s a cozy and creamy treat that can be served warm or cold, perfect for evoking the wizarding world.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cream soda
  • 1 tbsp butterscotch syrup
  • 2 tbsp whipped cream
  • Optional: pinch of salt or splash of butter extract

Instructions

  1. Stir butterscotch syrup into cream soda.
  2. Top with whipped cream.
  3. Heat gently for the warm version, or serve over ice for a cold version.
