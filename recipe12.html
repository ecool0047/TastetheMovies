Classic New Orleans Beignets

Description:

A real Southern staple, these fluffy, golden-fried beignets are dusted with powdered sugar and made famous in The Princess and the Frog.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup milk
  • 3½ cups flour
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Activate yeast in warm water with sugar and let sit for a few minutes.
  2. Add milk, egg, melted butter, and flour
