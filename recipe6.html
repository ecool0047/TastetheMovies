Kronk’s Spinach Puffs

Description:

A savory, flaky pastry filled with spinach, cheese, and a touch of garlic—just like Kronk’s famous creation!

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 cup spinach (fresh or frozen), cooked & squeezed dry
  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ¼ cup feta or parmesan
  • Garlic, nutmeg, salt (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Mix the cooked spinach, cream cheese, feta or parmesan, garlic, nutmeg, and salt to form the filling.
  2. Cut the puff pastry into squares and place a spoonful of filling in the center of each square.
  3. Fold the squares into triangles, sealing the edges.
  4. Brush the top of each puff with an egg wash.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 15–20 minutes until golden and crispy.
