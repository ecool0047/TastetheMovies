Salmon Roe Rice Bowl with Egg and Green Onion

Description:

This comforting and simple dish, inspired by the movie Ponyo, combines the delicate flavors of salmon roe, scrambled egg, and fresh green onions atop warm rice.

Ingredients

  • Cooked white rice
  • 1 egg, lightly scrambled
  • 2 tbsp salmon roe (ikura)
  • Green onion
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Place hot rice in a bowl.
  2. Top with scrambled egg, salmon roe, and green onions.
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce to taste.
