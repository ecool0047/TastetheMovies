Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 yellow squash
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Heat a pan and sauté mixture.
  4. Place in baking dish.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving.
