New Orleans-Style Shrimp & Sausage Gumbo

Description:

A rich, flavorful gumbo straight from Tiana's dream restaurant menu. Perfectly spiced with shrimp, sausage, and the traditional “holy trinity” of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • For roux: ½ cup flour + ½ cup oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced (the “holy trinity”)
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 smoked sausage, sliced
  • Cajun seasoning, to taste
  • 4 cups seafood or chicken broth
  • White rice (to serve)

Instructions

  1. Make the roux by combining flour and oil, stirring constantly until the mixture turns brown.
  2. Add the diced onion, bell pepper, and celery. Cook until the vegetables are soft.
  3. Add the broth, sausage, and Cajun seasoning. Simmer the mixture for a few minutes.
  4. Finally, add the shrimp and cook until just pink and cooked through.
  5. Serve the gumbo over hot white rice.
