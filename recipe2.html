English Meat Pie

Description:

A classic English meat pie featured in Sweeney Todd. Let's assume yours won't include any barbers!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Shortcrust pastry (store-bought or homemade)
  • Egg wash

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground meat in a pan.
  2. Add diced onions, carrots, and potatoes. Cook until vegetables soften.
  3. Pour in the beef broth, simmer until the mixture thickens.
  4. Line a pie pan with shortcrust pastry and pour the meat mixture in.
  5. Cover with the top crust and seal the edges. Brush with egg wash.
  6. Bake at 400Â°F for 30-35 minutes until golden.
