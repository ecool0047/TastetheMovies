Ratatouille

Description:

A visually stunning French vegetable dish made famous by Remy in Pixar's Ratatouille.

Thinly sliced vegetables baked over a rich garlic tomato base—simple, elegant, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 yellow squash
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Thyme

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Thinly slice all vegetables into rounds.
  3. Spread tomato sauce mixed with minced garlic in a baking dish.
  4. Arrange the sliced vegetables in a spiral pattern over the sauce.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with thyme.
  6. Cover with parchment paper and bake for 45-50 minutes.
