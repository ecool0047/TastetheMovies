Decadent Fudge Chocolate Cake

Description:

Yes, it's that one. Bruce's "punishment", a rich and fudgy chocolate cake made famous in the movie Matilda.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two round cake pans.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda) in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
  4. Slowly stir in hot coffee last. The batter will be thin.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Let the cakes cool before frosting with chocolate ganache or buttercream.
